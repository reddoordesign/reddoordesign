About us

Hello!

Hello, I'm a web and graphic designer based in London. I love clean, innovative, and beautiful designs. My background is in marketing but it was my love for design and coding that made me make the official switch to design in 2016. I now work as a freelance designer, creating everything from UX websites to illustrations and logos.
Working remotely has allowed me to keep exploring the world and continue my love for photography.Check me out on Instagram to see where I'm at.

Why work with me?
My background in marketing and degree in art, combined with my skills in tech has made me a well-rounded and innovative designer. I design and code my own sites using programing languages like HTML, CSS, PHP, JavaScript, and jQuery. I also create illustrations, design logos and other graphic and branding material. Excellent design is important for a website but understanding the marketing side and knowing how to create a site that will achieve its intended results is essential.
